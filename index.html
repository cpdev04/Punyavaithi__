KNOW MORE ABOUT

IDEOLOGICAL LEADER FOR YOUTH

Dr. Punyavathi is a well-known laparoscopic gynecologist in Bangalore who has treated numerous patients with exceptional care. People have been receiving healthcare for over 23 years, and various free medical camps have been held for the state's poor residents. As a doctor, she realized her strong desire to serve the country through her abilities in health and education. During the COVID pandemic, Dr. Punyavathi treated patients at such low charges that their financial demands were not compromised.

With this desire, she joined the Bhartiya Janta Party in Karnataka, and she is now ready to put her aspirations into action to make the world a better and healthier place for people.
